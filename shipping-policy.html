Shipping Policy

Overview

At Lumina Luxe, we are committed to delivering our curated collections to your doorstep with the utmost care and efficiency. Our global logistics network ensures that luxury is never more than a few days away.

Processing Times

All orders are processed within 1–2 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on weekends or public holidays.

If we are experiencing a high volume of orders, shipments may be delayed by a few days. Please allow additional days in transit for delivery.

Delivery Timeframes

Region Estimated Delivery
Portugal & Spain 2–4 business days
European Union 4–8 business days
United Kingdom 5–9 business days
United States & Canada 7–14 business days
Rest of World 10–20 business days

Shipping Costs

We are pleased to offer Complimentary Shipping on all orders over €75.

For orders below €75, a standard shipping fee starting from €4.90 will be applied at checkout, depending on the destination and weight of the parcel.

Order Tracking

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a Shipping Confirmation email containing your tracking number(s). The tracking number will be active within 24 hours.

Customs, Duties & Taxes

Lumina Luxe is not responsible for any customs and taxes applied to your order. All fees imposed during or after shipping are the responsibility of the customer (tariffs, taxes, etc.).

Lost or Damaged Orders

Lumina Luxe is not liable for any products damaged or lost during shipping. If you received your order damaged, please contact the shipment carrier to file a claim. Please save all packaging materials and damaged goods before filing a claim.

Returns & Exchanges

Our Return & Refund Policy provides detailed information about options and procedures for returning your order. We offer a 14-day return window for most items.

For any inquiries regarding shipping, please reach out to our concierge team at luminaluxe.space@gmail.com.
