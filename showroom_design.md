| Limestone Texture | Limestone (#E5DDD3) | Flooring (entire space) | *Neutral foundation, warm underfoot* |
| Fluted Glass | Frosted white | Room dividers between zones | *Soft light diffusion, privacy* |

### Material Wall (Circadian Suite)

A curated 8ft × 4ft wall with interchangeable panels showing how different light temperatures affect material perception:

| Panel | Material | Dawn (1800K) Effect | Noon (5000K) Effect | Dusk (2200K) Effect |
|-------|----------|---------------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| 1 | Carrara Marble | Warm honey patina | Crisp white veins | Deep amber richness |
| 2 | Charcoal Velvet | Soft, almost invisible texture | Deep textural contrast | Warm, enveloping glow |
| 3 | Fumed Oak | Rich mahogany tone | Natural grain pops | Deep, warm resonance |
| 4 | Brushed Brass | Golden reflection | Cool metallic clarity | Warm bronze richness |

## 5. Experience Flow

1. **Entry** — Guest arrives to a warm 1800K amber glow (Dawn scene). Palladiom keypad at reception.
2. **Ketra Experience** — Guide demonstrates full solar cycle: Dawn → Noon → Dusk in 60 seconds. Guest interacts with Palladiom keypad gallery.
3. **Circadian Mapping Suite** — Guest presses "Siri, show me Noon" button. Material Wall illuminates. 24-hour cycle accelerated.
4. **Crestron Demo** — Guest sees lighting + HVAC + shades working in perfect sync via 10" touch screen.
5. **Savant Corner** — Guest explores energy dashboard on Apple TV. Sees real-time consumption data.
6. **Consultation** — Seated area in reception. Custom lighting plan discussed.

---

## 6. Brand Aesthetic Notes

- **Invisible Technology:** All processors, wiring, dimmers in ventilated rack room
- **Zero visible wires:** Every fixture is plaster-in or cable-managed
- **Dark + Warm:** Obsidian walls make the light "pop" — fixtures appear to float
- **Scent:** Subtle Hinoki wood + amber diffuser
- **Sound:** Gentle 432Hz ambient through invisible architectural speakers
